A gram of COâ‚‚ is a tiny amount - you'd need about 2,000 grams to fill a 1-liter bottle. The average web user generates around 75g daily from browsing alone. Across billions of internet users, this adds up to a significant contributor to climate change.

What we track

CurbYourCarbon measures your client-side carbon footprint - the data transferred over the network and your device's energy consumption while browsing. We don't include server-side infrastructure like data centers, which would add 2-10x more depending on the service.
